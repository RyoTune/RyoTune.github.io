1. Click the **Manage Mods** button.

<Image src={img_re_manage_mods} alt="Reloaded's Manage Mods Button" />

2. Click the **New** button to create a new mod.

<Image src={img_re_manage_mods_new} alt="Manage Mods New Button" />

3. Enter a unique **Mod ID** for your mod. There are no requirements, but something like `Game.ModName` is recommended such as `P5R.CostumeFramework`.

<Image src={img_re_create_id} alt="Creating a mod, setting a Mod ID." />

4. Enter the basic details of your mod like its name, description, and version. Add an icon by clicking the **Reloaded icon** and selecting a new image.

<Image
  src={img_re_create_common}
  alt="Creating a Mod, setting common details."
/>

5. [Add any **Mod Dependencies** your mod has](/guides/reloaded/02-mod-dependency). **Mod Dependencies** are mods that your mod **relies on** to work.

<Image src={img_re_create_dep} alt="Creating a Mod, adding mod dependencies." />

6. (Optional) [Add **Update Support** to your mod](/guides/reloaded/03-mod-updates). This lets **Reloaded** know whenever you release a new version and prompt your users to install it.

<Image
  src={img_re_create_update}
  alt="Creating a Mod, adding update support."
/>

7. Add any games your mod supports, usually just one. This determines whether your mod will show up in a game's **Mods List**.

<Image
  src={img_re_create_finish}
  alt="Creating a Mod, adding supported games."
/>

8. Go to your game's **Mods List** and enable your new mod.

<Image src={img_re_create_enable} alt="Creating a Mod, enable your new mod." />

9. Edit your mod at any time by **right-clicking** it in the **Mods List** and selecting **Edit Mod**.

<Image
  src={img_re_mods_edit}
  alt="Edit a mod by right-clicking it in the Mods List."
/>

10. Open your mod's folder by **right-clicking** it and selecting **Open Folder**. This is where all your **mod's files** will be placed.
    - Your mod's folder can be found at: `RELOADED_II/Mods/MOD_ID/`, where `RELOADED_II` is **Reloaded's** install folder.

<Image src={img_re_mods_open} alt="Open a mod's folder by right-clicking it." />

<Image src={img_re_mod_folder} alt="Empty mod folder." />

11. Finished! ðŸŽ‰
